Colt Gaa Club are hosting a benefit night for a long serving player in Doogue's pub Raheen on Saturday the 7th May. 
The purpose being to support this individual in their battle against cancer and the long journey ahead. The night promises to
be full of craic  and laughter, with music, finger food and a raffle to be held throughout the event. Donations can be made on
the night or via the goFundMe (page linked here). We hope to see a packed venue for this worthy cause, thanks in advance for your support.
